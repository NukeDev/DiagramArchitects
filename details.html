Project intro

Refurbishment of a private residence in a converted Victorian school building.

The project brief was to transform the existing basement into a space for the client to entertain guests, making spatial organisation a top priority. This was accomplished through the addition of strategic lighting to fit within the desired minimalist aesthetic to create a truly artistic design complete with bespoke furniture.

The custom designed staircases were a key design feature fostering continuity throughout the house and projecting a sense of openness.

This aesthetic was carried through to the bedroom where the original ceiling beams were exposed. Timeless steel girders bring in an industrial element with respect to the history of the building, and blend seamlessly into the modern clean lines of the design.

Client: Private Residence

Location: Battersea, Southwest London

SQ M: 210

Status: Completed
